About MCLAU

Since 2019, MCLAU Solutions Pty Ltd has been manufacturing and supplying a unique range of detergents to end users. We are a 100% Youth, 100% Women & 100% Black owned company based in Northern Cape, Kathu with an extended footprint in Gauteng.

We specialize in manufacturing and distributing detergent products, cleaning materials, chemicals and paper products to retailers and customers.

Our products are tested at Tshwane University of Technology and technology innovation agency in line with SARS Quality Standards. We believe in "core" business, "COST and RESULT", delivering products directly to your door personally or through couriers.
