About MCLAU Solutions

100% Youth, 100% Women & 100% Black owned company delivering excellence in chemical formulation and cleaning solutions since 2019

Our Story

Since 2019, MCLAU Solutions Pty Ltd has been manufacturing and supplying a very unique range of detergents to the end user. At MCLAU Solutions we manufacture our own range of products on site where we ensure that our clients receive only the highest quality & brightest results.

MCLAU Solutions is 100% Youth, 100% Women & 100% Black owned company. The company is based in Northern Cape, Kathu with an extended footprint in Gauteng in association with an established company with the capacity for research, development, manufacturing, warehousing and distribution.

We specialize in the manufacturing and distribution of detergents products / Cleaning Materials / chemical and paper products to retailers and customers. Not only do we manufacture but we also supply cleaning consumables, cleaning and maintenance services of i.e Workshops, plants, offices, households, etc.

Our products are tested at Tshwane University of Technology, technology innovation agency inline with SARS Quality Standards. We believe in "core" business, "COST and RESULT", delivering products directly to your door personally or through couriers.

Vision & Mission

Our company vision is to expand our horizons and to make our company the leader in manufacturing and supply of cleaning detergents and industrial cleaning products. We aim to provide quality service to all our customers' needs while maintaining our commitment to sustainability and innovation.

Our Mission
