OUR LEGACY

Precision Chemical Engineering Since 2019

MCLAU Solutions Pty Ltd manufactures and supplies a unique range of detergents, industrial chemicals, and cleaning solutions to end users across South Africa. Every formulation is tested at Tshwane University of Technology and aligned with SARS quality standards.

Based in Northern Cape, Kathu, with an extended footprint in Gauteng, we combine R&D, manufacturing, warehousing, and distribution. We believe in "core" business, cost and result — delivering directly to your door personally or via courier.
