Who We Are

ABOUT MCLAU

Since 2019, MCLAU Solutions Pty Ltd has been manufacturing and supplying a unique range of detergents to end users. We are a 100% Youth, 100% Women & 100% Black owned company.

We specialise in manufacturing and distributing detergent products, cleaning materials, chemicals and paper products to retailers and customers across South Africa.

Our products are tested at Tshwane University of Technology and Technology Innovation Agency, aligned with SARS Quality Standards.
