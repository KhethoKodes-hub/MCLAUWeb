Our Location

MCLAU Solutions is based in Kathu, Northern Cape with extended operations in Gauteng. Visit us at our headquarters or contact us to arrange a meeting at your premises.

Frequently Asked Questions

What areas do you serve? +
We are based in Kathu, Northern Cape but serve clients across South Africa. We have an extended footprint in Gauteng through our partnerships and can arrange delivery and services nationwide.
How quickly can you deliver products? +
Standard delivery times are 3-5 business days for most areas. For urgent orders, we offer expedited shipping options. Local customers in Kathu can often receive same-day delivery.
Can you customize chemical formulations? +
Yes! We specialize in developing specific formulas to suit customer requirements. Our partnership with research facilities allows us to create custom solutions for unique challenges.
What quality standards do you follow? +
Our products are tested at Tshwane University of Technology and technology innovation agency in line with SARS Quality Standards. We are committed to continuous improvement and compliance with industry requirements.
Do you offer emergency cleaning services? +
Yes, we provide emergency response cleaning services for situations like spills, accidents, or urgent maintenance needs. Contact us directly for emergency situations and we'll prioritize your request.
